2020.11.11

Many thanks to the invitation of Aristide Antonas, Jimenez Lai delivered a lecture about life on a desert island at ETH. There are reflections back to our Tuvalu efforts with David Jimenez Iniesta and Kevin Pazik, as well as the artic stories with Ted Miaojie Zhang. We also showed some of the newer stories about a possible post-COVID Downtown Los Angeles.

2020.10.21

Over Zoom space at the University of New Mexico, Jimenez Lai delivers a lecture about the status of life inside of a medium that is inside of another medium.

2020.10.09

Meanwhile at SCI-arc - Jimenez Lai sits down with Erin Besler to discuss the status of sunsets. This event is hosted by Tom Wiscombe, and it was an unveiling of our summer project Flying Fleet of Social Zones.

2020.08.01

Jimenez Lai joins Cruz Garcia and Nathalie Frankowski for the Loudreaders series. This has been a quick check-in on the status of Metamodernism.

2020.07.24

Meanwhile at SCAD, Jimenez Lai sits down (via Zoom) with Nadia Swarovski and Alexandra Cunningham to discuss the status of collaboration. We had a chance to revisit the 2017 Designer of the Future project Terrazzo Palazzo. Thank you so much for the invitation!

2020.06.25

Whatever Happened to the Pritzker Prize? Filipe Magalhaes from atelier fala sits down with Jimenez Lai to discuss the status of the Pritzker over the past 30 years. Patterns, as well as the waves of changes in the times, produced several names that we scratch our heads over, but also names that we deeply admire. Thank you to the London Architecture Foundation for hosting this event via Zoom as a part of the 100 Day Studio project!

2020.05.29

Thanks to POOL Magazine (UCLA), we had a chance to chat with Outpost Office from Columbus Ohio alongside Garret Ricciardi and Mira Henry.

2020.05.26

Meanwhile in Geneve, CH - Jimenez Lai joins Ana Luisa Soares from atelier fala on a review at HEAD. What a refreshing experience being around people who really know how to put a building together. No rhetorical devices needed, just spatial complexity.
